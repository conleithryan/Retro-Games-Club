About The Website

This website was built to connect retro gaming fans across the Munster Technological University campuses. Whether you love the SNES, the Sega Genesis, or classic arcade cabinets, this is your home base.

Why Join the Club?

  • Meet like-minded students and make new friends.
  • Access to our library of retro consoles and games.
  • Participate in monthly tournaments with prizes.
  • Pizza and snacks at every LAN party!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own a console to join?

No! We provide all consoles and controllers at our events. Just bring yourself!

Is there a membership fee?

Membership is free for all current MTU students.

Can I bring my own games?

Yes, we encourage members to bring their favourite cartridges or discs to share.
